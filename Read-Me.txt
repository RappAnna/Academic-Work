Read-Me
IBES_Forecast_EPS_Quarterly_Individual
--> Data: IBES EPS Forecasts of individual forecasters with quarterly frequency, adjusted for stock splits, for all US-firms
- pulls WRDS quarterly individual forecasts of EPS
- linking CUSIPs with permno from CRSP
- flagging each observation whether in SP500 or not
- keep for each permno, target quarter, forecast horizon and estimator the most recent estimate in a quarter
- create forecast error and forecast revision

IBES_Forecast_LTG_Quarterly_Individual
--> Data: IBES LTG of earnings of individual forecasters with quarterly frequency, for all US-firms
- pulls WRDS quarterly individual forecasts of LTG of earnings
- linking CUSIPs with permno from CRSP
- flagging each observation whether in SP500 or not
- keep for each permno and each date when forecast was done the most recent quarterly observation
- calculate 1-year changes in the forecast for growth in earnings between years t and t-1 as forecast revision

IBES_Forecast_EPS_LTG_Merge_Quarterly_Individual
--> Data: dataframes of file "IBES_Forecast_EPS_Quarterly_Individual" and "IBES_Forecast_LTG_Quarterly_Individual"
- merge both dataframes to one which gives overview over
   a) quarterly estimates of EPS on individual basis for each permno as well as forecast revision and error of the EPS
   b) forecasted LTG for the same estimator and permno where the forecast date of LTG is same as forecast date of EPS
- merge based on the forecast date of the EPS: forecast of date is +/- 1 month of forecast date of EPS
